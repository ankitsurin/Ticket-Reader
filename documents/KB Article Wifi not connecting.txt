Issue : Wi-Fi Not Connecting  
Troubleshooting Steps:  
1. Restart your router and modem by unplugging them for 30 seconds and plugging them back in.  
2. Restart your computer or mobile device.  
3. Check if other devices can connect to the Wi-Fi network.  
4. Ensure that the Wi-Fi is enabled on your device.  
5. Forget the network and reconnect by entering the correct password.  
6. Move closer to the router to avoid signal issues.  
7. Update the network drivers (for computers) or restart network settings.  
8. If the problem persists, contact your internet service provider.
